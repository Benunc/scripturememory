# Version 1.6.3 - Armor Challenge

This patch release adds the "Armor Challenge" verse set featuring Ephesians 6:10-20, the famous "Armor of God" passage, providing users with a powerful spiritual warfare memorization challenge using the ESV translation.

## New Features

### Armor Challenge
- **Added Ephesians 6:10-20** as a new verse set option
- **ESV translation** for accurate and reliable biblical text
- **Spiritual warfare focus** with the complete Armor of God passage
- **Consistent with existing chapter challenges** (Romans 8, Philippians 1, Philippians 2)
- **Available in both frontend and backend** for seamless integration

## Content Details

### Verse Set Information
- **Name**: Armor Challenge
- **Reference**: Ephesians 6:10-20
- **Translation**: English Standard Version (ESV)
- **Verse Count**: 1 (complete passage as single unit)
- **Content**: The complete "Armor of God" passage

### Key Themes in Ephesians 6:10-20
- **Spiritual Warfare** - Understanding the nature of spiritual battles
- **The Armor of God** - Six pieces of spiritual protection:
  - **Belt of Truth** - Foundation of honesty and integrity
  - **Breastplate of Righteousness** - Protection of the heart through righteous living
  - **Shoes of the Gospel of Peace** - Readiness to share the gospel
  - **Shield of Faith** - Protection against spiritual attacks
  - **Helmet of Salvation** - Assurance of salvation protecting the mind
  - **Sword of the Spirit** - The Word of God as offensive weapon
- **Prayer and Perseverance** - The importance of constant prayer and alertness
