## For Group Administrators

### How to Use
1. **Navigate to group details** as a group leader or super admin
2. **Select "Assign Verse Set"** from the admin options
3. **Choose from complete list** of available verse sets including:
   - All chapter challenges (Romans 8, Philippians 1, Philippians 2)
   - Armor Challenge (Ephesians 6:10-20)
   - Standard verse sets (Default, Children's, GPC Youth)
4. **Assign to individual members** or all group members at once

### Available Assignments
- **Individual member assignment** - Select specific member and verse set
- **Bulk assignment** - Assign verse set to all group members
- **Complete verse set access** - All challenges and standard sets available
